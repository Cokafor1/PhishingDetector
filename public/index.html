About Phish Me
Built to slow down the moment where phishing usually wins.
We made this tool to help people read suspicious email more carefully instead of relying on instinct alone. Phish Me combines sender analysis, content heuristics, and engine reputation so sketchy messages stand out faster while normal business email stays readable and calm.
Why it exists Inbox threats often look routine. This report is meant to turn vague suspicion into clear signals you can act on.
How we tune it We keep pushing the detector toward real-world email scams like fake HR notices, payment lures, and credential harvesting without over-flagging ordinary work mail.
